Professional Profile

Evangelos Kastamoulas has been Mad Dog Athletics Spinning Program and SpinPower Master Instructor.
He is certified in indoor cycling from ICA(Indoor Cycling Association), Power Cycling (Studio One).

He also possesses cycling certifications for training Children, pregnants the elderly etc.

He has been a distinguished cyclist since 1991 with achievements in the Greek Cycling Championship, he has participated in Asca Racing, etc.
He's also been a Greek National team member and also participated in the long distance race Paris–Brest-Paris, in which he did 1230 km in 64 hours, without any stops.

Cycling coach, Articles writer, Startrac Bike technician, Dee Jay and spinning Presenter in Greece, as well as abroad (Qatar, UK, Holland, Belgium, Miami etc.)
